Overline is a brand and UI design studio. We help organizations grow by aligning people through brand strategy, visual identity, and digital design.

Process

All projects are rooted in strategy and story. We’ll come to a shared understanding of your goals and what the design needs to accomplish.

From there, we’ll work to design a solution that is beautiful and honors the strategy we align on. There will be the chance to iterate towards the final solution through revisions and collaboration.

Finally, I’ll hand off the design work back to you along with any education or guidelines for success.
