For Everyone

Bring everyone on campus closer

Access for everyone—staff, faculty, and students. From students just getting started with your online courses to board members checking in for a donations update, Populi gives everyone what they need—wherever they are, whatever device.

Academic Services

Provide the best experience for students by giving them access to their academic information in an easy-to-use interface. Support other teams by providing reports and access to the information they need to get their jobs done.

Features

  • Work with admissions to approve transfer courses
  • Degree Audit
  • Academic Calendar
  • Academic Discipline
  • Registration, Transcript & Grade Locks
  • Advising Tools

IT Teams

Offload tasks to Populi so you can focus on meeting your school’s evolving needs. Data is accessible for whatever project you tackle next.

Features

  • Web API
  • Webhooks
  • Integrations
  • Canvas, Moodle, GSuite, Big Blue Button, Unicheck & more

Faculty

Stay in touch with students via email, bulletin boards and SMS notifications. Everything you need to run engaging and semi-automated courses. Submit grades at the click of a button.

Features

  • Rubrics and automated grading
  • Online testing with proctoring
  • Live streaming & file hosting
  • Discussions & Groups
  • Groups

Students

Check your grades, pay a bill, register for a course or participate in an course discussion... all from any device at any time. Keep in touch with everything and everyone that is part of your college experience.

Features

  • Single login and profile
  • Online registration & bill pay
  • Mobile apps
  • Groups and messaging
  • Access to transcript, degree audit and more

Fundraising

Set goals and work together with your supporters to acheive them. Build your mailing lists and use preset communication plans to stay in touch while keeping manual work to a minimum.

Features

  • Donor CRM
  • Campaign management
  • Online & mobile donations
  • Giving receipts and annual statements
  • Custom donation pages
  • Bulk Communications: SMS, Email & Print

Billing

Registration triggers charges automatically based on preset tuition schedules and fee rules. Bulk tasks and triggers allow you to automate billing as much as possible. Online opt-in recurring payment plans make it easy for students to pay and for you to stay on top of your receivables.

Features

  • Recurring payment plans
  • Stripe & Authorize.net payment gateways
  • 1098-T, T2202A, T4A
  • Enrollment agreements
  • Check printing

Marketing

Prospects, students, parents of students, alumni, donors... everyone at your fingertips. Communication features help you get the word out and triggers help keep the manual work to a minimum.

Features

  • Communication plans
  • Email, SMS & mail merge
  • Templating
  • Automated to-do lists

Advising

A clear picture of how each of your advisees are doing so that you can help them succeed. See which courses they have taken and what they still need to complete their degree. Custom flags let you know if someone is missing class or falling behind academically.

Features

  • Customizable flags
  • Enrollment & grade locks
  • One-click degree audits

Executives

Colleges have many moving parts and you need to stay on top of your key indicators. Give staff clear objectives and check in on progress, ensuring you give students the best possible environment for success.

Features

  • Dashboards
  • Alerts and notifications
  • Change logs
  • Role-based user access

Financial Aid

Finally you can run financial aid alongside your admissions and billing teams. No more trying to import/export data. Everything just works so that by the time your students are enrolling, their financial aid is ready to roll.

Features

  • Download and import SAIG files
  • Aid applications
  • Bulk tasks and automations
  • Push updates to COD
  • SAP Reporting
  • Integrated with student billing
